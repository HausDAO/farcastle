# Values

**Onchain**: Harnessing the magic of Ethereum, we ensure every action is recorded and visible, empowering communities with transparency and trust on their collective journey.

**Open Source**: Devoted to the ancient art of collaborative crafting, we embrace shared knowledge, ensuring resources and spells are freely exchanged.

**Decentralized**: We uphold the principles of anti-censorship and permissionless access, where every voice holds power and each decision shapes our fate.

**Collective Action**: Farcastle champions noble quests to achieve shared goals, fostering unity to drive meaningful impact.

**Built in Public**: Weaving tales of our endeavors in the light of day, we seek to engage the community and allow feedback to guide our way.
